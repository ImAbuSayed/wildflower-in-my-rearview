[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
You're a wildflower blooming in my rearview
A midnight dream that I'm driving into
Got that slow-burn heat in your quiet smile
Make me want to pull over and stay a while

[Verse 1]
The dashboard lights are glowing soft and low
Got nowhere to be and everywhere to go
You tilt your head and let your hair fall down
The most beautiful secret in this dusty town

[Pre-Chorus]
I can feel the tension in the space between
Like a movie scene playing on a screen
Your fingertips are tracing on my sleeve
Got me begging for a moment that we'll never leave

[Chorus]
Oh, you're the velvet warmth in the evening air
I'm caught in the gravity of your stare
Just a slow, sweet drive with nothing left to hide
My beautiful wildflower riding by my side

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, riding by my side, yeah
You're the only destination on this midnight ride

[Verse 2]

Watching the shadows and the streetlights pass
You lean in close and I can feel your breath
A tender kind of ruin, scaring me to death
